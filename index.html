About

Hi, I’m Auden.

I’m an enthusiastic digital communications professional with a passion for storytelling and creating meaningful content for good. With experience in Adobe Creative Suite, social media strategy, graphic design, email marketing, video production, and photography, I create campaigns that align with organizational goals and values.

Currently serving as a Communications Assistant at Seattle Preparatory School, I’ve supported social media growth, admissions and advancement marketing, and community-building initiatives with DEI at the forefront of my work. When I’m not behind the camera or keyboard, I’m dedicated to building community and being a positive force. Oh—and I’m from Texas. Yee-haw.
